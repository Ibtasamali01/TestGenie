### **3 Groom Levels Available:**
1. **🔍 Insight** (Balanced Groom)
   - Balanced analysis — highlights clarity, ACs, QA scenarios
   - Ideal for refinement meetings and sprint grooming

2. **⚡ Actionable** (QA + DoR Coaching)
